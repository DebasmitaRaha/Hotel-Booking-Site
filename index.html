ABOUT US

Uncover Our Soulful Haven

Nestled on the banks of the serene Lake, our heritage hotel in Udaipur offers a glimpse into the opulence and grandeur of India's royal past!! Housed in a meticulously restored palace, the hotel features stunning architecture with intricate frescoes, marble carvings, and grand courtyards, gives hint of our rich cultural heritage. Our Hotel an ideal destination for tourists seeking an authentic and regal experience in Udaipur. Whether you are here to explore the rich history or simply to relax in a luxurious setting, our hotel promises an unforgettable stay, where the past seamlessly blends with the present, and every guest is treated with the utmost care and attention.
